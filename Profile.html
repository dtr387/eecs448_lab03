Profile: Cueball

Cueball is a stick figure from the xkcd comic series written by Randall Munroe.
Cueball is distinguished as a character by having no distinguishing features such as a hat, or hair. Cueball first made his appearance in xkcd comic 6.
Cueball is distinguished in many arts including but not limited to:

TimeTravel, Space travel, computer science, minecraft.
